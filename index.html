Welcome

Histori of ethic

Ethic is one of the dynamic theater performance group in Bangladesh established by a bunch of young blood in 2009. Ethic is the short form of “ASHO THEATER KORI”. As a member of group theater federation Ethic is moving forward with the spirit of the great liberation war through the production of plays for the betterment of people and society. Emphasizing creativity, Ethic is committed to presenting different relevant plays that suit the test and preferences of the audience.
